🔥 Fire Rune
Inscribing: Weapon or armor. Lasts until used.
Activate (reaction on hit): Target makes DC 20 STR save or is restrained. Takes 2d6 fire at start of each turn. Repeats save each turn. Lasts 1 min or until freed (STR DC 20).

🪨 Stone Rune
Inscribing: Weapon or armor. Lasts until used.
Activate (reaction, 30 ft): Target makes DC 20 WIS save or falls charmed for 1 min (incapacitated, speed 0). Repeats save each turn. No harm to end. Recharges after short/long rest.
Passive: Darkvision 120 ft.
⛰ Hill Rune
Inscribing: Weapon or armor. Lasts until used.
Activate (bonus action): Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage for 1 minute. Recharges after short/long rest.
Passive: Advantage on STR checks and saves against being moved or knocked prone.
☁ Cloud Rune
Inscribing: Weapon or armor. Lasts until used.
Activate (reaction, hit redirected): When you or a creature you can see within 30 ft is hit, redirect the attack to another creature within 30 ft (DM's call on validity). Recharges after short/long rest.
Passive: Double proficiency on Sleight of Hand and Deception checks.
⚡ Storm Rune
Inscribing: Weapon or armor. Lasts until used.
Activate (bonus action): Enter prophetic state for 1 min: can't be surprised, advantage on attack rolls/ability checks/saving throws if you roll <10 (use 10 instead). Recharges after short/long rest.
Passive: Advantage on Arcana checks.
🛡 Runic Shield
Reaction: When another creature you can see within 60 ft is hit, force the attacker to reroll the attack. Use the lower roll.
Uses: / Recharges after long rest.

⚡ Giant's Might

Activation: Bonus action. Duration: 1 minute.

  • Your size becomes Large (if space allows).
  • Advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.
  • Add 1d10 to ONE weapon attack damage per turn.
  • Your reach increases by 5 ft.
  • Uses per Long Rest: Equal to Proficiency Bonus (6 at current level).

⛏ Rockman (Dwarf) Racial Traits

Rockman Toughness

HP maximum increases by 1, and increases by 1 every time you gain a level. (+20 HP at current level)

Rockman Resilience

Advantage on saving throws against poison. Resistance against poison damage.

Stonecunning

INT (History) checks related to the origin of stonework: treated as proficient, add double Proficiency Bonus instead of normal Proficiency Bonus.

Gold Blood

Immune to disease. Does not age naturally.

Darkvision (Racial)

Can see in dim light as bright light, and in darkness as dim light, to a distance of 60 feet (120 ft via Stone Rune passive).

Numa (20 Points)

Mystical life-force. Current points tracked above. Can push CON above 22 for one round using Numa. Current: 20 / 20.

  • Action Surge (2/rest): Take one additional action on your turn.
  • Indomitable (3/long rest): Reroll a failed saving throw, must use new roll.
