⚡ Giant's Might

Activation: Bonus action. Duration: 1 minute.

  • Your size becomes Large (if space allows).
  • Advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.
  • Add 1d10 to ONE weapon attack damage per turn.
  • Your reach increases by 5 ft.
  • Uses per Long Rest: Equal to Proficiency Bonus (6 at current level).

⛏ Rockman (Dwarf) Racial Traits

Rockman Toughness

HP maximum increases by 1, and increases by 1 every time you gain a level. (+20 HP at current level)

Rockman Resilience

Advantage on saving throws against poison. Resistance against poison damage.

Stonecunning

INT (History) checks related to the origin of stonework: treated as proficient, add double Proficiency Bonus instead of normal Proficiency Bonus.

Gold Blood

Immune to disease. Does not age naturally.

Darkvision (Racial)

Can see in dim light as bright light, and in darkness as dim light, to a distance of 60 feet (120 ft via Stone Rune passive).

Numa (20 Points)

Mystical life-force. Current points tracked above. Can push CON above 22 for one round using Numa. Current: 20 / 20.

⚔ Special Combat Features & Abilities

Fighter Features

  • Fighting Style — Archery: +2 to ranged attack rolls.
  • Second Wind: Bonus action, regain 1d10 + Fighter level HP. 1/short or long rest.
  • Action Surge (2/rest): Take one additional action on your turn.
  • Indomitable (3/long rest): Reroll a failed saving throw, must use new roll.
  • Extra Attack (4): Attack 4 times when taking the Attack action.

Advantage & Special Rolls

  • ADV Insight (WIS) checks
  • ADV Arcana (INT) checks
  • ADV STR checks & saves when Giant's Might active
  • ADV STR checks & saves Hill Rune passive (vs. forced movement & prone)
  • DISADV Perception checks when Dwarven Thrower is stationary

Special Equipment & Magic

  • Magical Horn (Valkyrie Call):
    • Activate to summon a Valkyrie to fight alongside you in battle.
    • Consequence: After the battle ends, you must immediately fight the Valkyrie.
  • Numa — CON Push: Spend Numa to push CON above 22 for one round. Effect: CON exceeds normal cap of 22, boosting CON modifier, HP temp, concentration saves, etc.
